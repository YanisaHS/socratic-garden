# Skill: Interview one question at a time

When you need input from the human, ask **one focused question at a time**.

- Ask the single most important open question first.
- Wait for the answer before asking the next question.
- Do not present a long questionnaire unless the human explicitly asks for one.
- Prefer specific, answerable questions over broad ones ("Which audiences see
  this error?" rather than "Tell me everything about the users").
- If several questions are genuinely blocking, say so and still lead with the
  one that unblocks the most.
- Reflect the answer back briefly before moving on, so the human can correct any
  misunderstanding early.

The goal is a conversation the human can steer, not an interrogation.
